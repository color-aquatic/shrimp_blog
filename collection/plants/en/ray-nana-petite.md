# Anubias Nana Petite

## Introduction

Anubias Nana Petite is a tiny Anubias cultivar with compact leaves, ideal for nano tanks and foreground details.

## Growing Conditions

| Parameter | Recommended Range |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Temperature | 20-28C |
| pH | 6.0-7.8 |
| Hardness | Soft to hard |
| Lighting | Low to medium |
| CO2 | Optional |

## Notes

- Do not bury the rhizome.
- Tie to stone or driftwood.
- Remove old leaves to promote healthy new growth.
