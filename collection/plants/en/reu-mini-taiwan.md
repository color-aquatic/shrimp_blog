# Mini Taiwan Moss

## Introduction

Mini Taiwan Moss is a very small aquatic moss that creates compact green carpets in the foreground. It is popular in nano aquascapes.

## Growing Conditions

| Parameter | Recommended Range |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Temperature | 22-28C |
| pH | 5.0-8.0 |
| Hardness | Soft to hard |
| Lighting | Medium |
| CO2 | Optional |

## Notes

- Tie to mesh, rock, or wood for best control.
- Trim regularly to keep a clean carpet form.
- Avoid very strong flow in early growth.
