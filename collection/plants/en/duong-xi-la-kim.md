# Needle Leaf Fern

## Introduction

Needle Leaf Fern is a narrow-leaf form of Java Fern that adds a soft, flowing texture to aquascapes.

## Growing Conditions

| Parameter | Recommended Range |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Temperature | 18-30C |
| pH | 6.0-7.8 |
| Hardness | Soft to hard |
| Lighting | Low to medium |
| CO2 | Optional |

## Notes

- Keep the rhizome above substrate.
- Place in gentle to moderate flow.
- Trim to maintain shape and density.
