# Anubias Pinto

## Introduction

Anubias Pinto is a variegated Anubias with striking white and green marbling. It works well as a focal accent plant.

## Growing Conditions

| Parameter | Recommended Range |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Temperature | 20-28C |
| pH | 6.0-7.5 |
| Hardness | Soft to hard |
| Lighting | Medium |
| CO2 | Recommended |

## Notes

- Growth is slow and prefers stable conditions.
- Avoid excessive direct light to reduce algae risk.
- Keep rhizome above substrate.
