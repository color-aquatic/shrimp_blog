# Pelia Moss

## Introduction

Pelia Moss (Monosolenium tenerum) is a liverwort-like aquatic plant with a unique texture and shape. It is often used as a natural accent in aquascapes.

## Growing Conditions

| Parameter | Recommended Range |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Temperature | 18-26C |
| pH | 5.5-7.5 |
| Hardness | Soft to medium |
| Lighting | Medium to high |
| CO2 | Recommended |

## Notes

- Do not bury in substrate.
- Attach loosely to hardscape or let it drift in calm zones.
- Remove old fronds to prevent debris buildup.
