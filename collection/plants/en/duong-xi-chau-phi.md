# African Fern

## Introduction

African Fern (Bolbitis heudelotii) is a classic rhizome plant known for its deeply divided leaves and elegant texture.

## Growing Conditions

| Parameter | Recommended Range |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Temperature | 20-28C |
| pH | 5.0-7.5 |
| Hardness | Soft to medium |
| Lighting | Medium |
| CO2 | Recommended |

## Notes

- Never bury the rhizome.
- Attach to wood or rock.
- Keep gentle water movement around the plant.
