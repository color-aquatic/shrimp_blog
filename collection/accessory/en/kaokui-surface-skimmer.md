## Application and Usage

### Functions:
- Remove oil film on water surface
- Clean air bubbles and floating debris
- Increase gas exchange at surface
- Improve clarity and aesthetics of tank

### Installation Guide:
1. Mount device to corner or tank wall
2. Adjust appropriate suction depth
3. Connect to main filtration system
4. Check operation and flow rate

### Maintenance:
- Clean suction surface regularly
- Check connecting pipes
- Clean removable components
- Replace parts when necessary

### Suitable for:
- Planted aquariums
- Premium shrimp tanks
- Mini fish tanks, nano tanks
- Closed aquatic systems
