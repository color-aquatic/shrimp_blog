# Kaokui Waterfall Filter

## Introduction

The Kaokui Waterfall Filter is a premium hang-on-back filtration system with unique waterfall design. The product comes in two main models KK-528 and KK-538, featuring a slim design that saves space while maintaining high filtration efficiency.

## Features

- **Brand**: Kaokui
- **Model**: KK-528, KK-538
- **Design**: Slim Hanging Filter
- **Style**: Natural waterfall flow
- **Installation**: Hang-on-back


## Application and Usage

### Advantages:
- Creates natural waterfall flow
- Increases dissolved oxygen in water
- Space-saving hang-on-back design
- Smooth operation, no noise

### Installation Guide:
1. Hang the filter on the outside of the tank wall
2. Adjust appropriate height
3. Check water flow and flow rate
4. Regular cleaning of filter media
