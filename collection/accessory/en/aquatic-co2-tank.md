# Aquatic CO2 Tank

## Introduction

The Aquatic CO2 Tank is a device that supplies carbon dioxide for aquatic plants, helping to promote photosynthesis and growth of underwater plants. This is essential equipment for professional planted aquariums.

## Features

- **Function**: CO2 supply for aquascaping
- **Application**: Planted aquariums, shrimp tanks with plants
- **Effectiveness**: Rapid plant growth
- **Safety**: Pressure regulator, gauge included
- **Easy to use**: Simple installation

## Application and Usage

### Benefits:
- Aquatic plants develop strongly, green and fresh
- Creates oxygen-rich environment
- Reduces algae, improves water quality
- Enhances natural beauty of the tank

### Installation Guide:
1. Attach pressure regulator to CO2 tank
2. Connect silicone tubing
3. Install CO2 diffuser in the tank
4. Adjust appropriate flow rate
5. Regular inspection and maintenance

### Safety Notes:
- Check pressure regularly
- Keep away from heat sources
- Transport carefully to avoid impacts
- Replace tank at proper intervals
