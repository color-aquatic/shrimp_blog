# Aquatic Moss Glue

## Introduction

GUO Elephant Super Glue for aquatic moss is a specialized product for decorating and fixing moss and aquatic plants in fish and shrimp tanks. The product is safe for aquatic life and has strong bonding ability in water environment.

## Features

- **Brand**: GUO Elephant
- **Type**: Aquatic Super Glue
- **Properties**: Waterproof, biologically safe
- **Application**: Moss bonding, aquatic plants
- **Features**: Quick dry, strong bonding

## Application and Usage

### Functions:
- Fix Java moss, Phoenix moss to stones, wood
- Attach small aquatic plants to decorative positions
- Create natural moss carpets
- Repair broken decorative items

### Usage Instructions:
1. Clean the surface to be bonded
2. Dry completely before bonding
3. Apply glue thinly and evenly
4. Press firmly for 30-60 seconds
5. Wait for complete drying before placing in water

### Safety Notes:
- Do not swallow
- Avoid contact with eyes
- Wash hands after use
- Store away from children
