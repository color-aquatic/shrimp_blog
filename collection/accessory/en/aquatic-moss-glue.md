## Application and Usage

### Functions:
- Fix Java moss, Phoenix moss to stones, wood
- Attach small aquatic plants to decorative positions
- Create natural moss carpets
- Repair broken decorative items

### Usage Instructions:
1. Clean the surface to be bonded
2. Dry completely before bonding
3. Apply glue thinly and evenly
4. Press firmly for 30-60 seconds
5. Wait for complete drying before placing in water

### Safety Notes:
- Do not swallow
- Avoid contact with eyes
- Wash hands after use
- Store away from children
