## Application and Usage

### Functions:
- Decompose excess food and fish/shrimp waste
- Reduce harmful ammonia and nitrite
- Balance microorganism system in tank
- Improve water clarity
- Enhance health of aquatic life

### Usage Instructions:
1. **New tanks**: Add bacteria 1-2 weeks before introducing fish/shrimp
2. **Existing tanks**: Supplement regularly every 1-2 weeks
3. **Dosage**: Follow package instructions
4. **Mixing**: Dissolve in tank water before adding

### Usage timing:
- When starting new tanks
- After cleaning filters
- When water quality decreases
- Regular maintenance for microorganism system

### Notes:
- Do not use with antibiotics
- Avoid direct sunlight when mixing
- Close tightly after use
- Check expiration date
