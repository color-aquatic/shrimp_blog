# Neo-Helios Light

## Introduction

The Neo-Helios Flat XP Series Solar Light RGB 3 in 1 is a premium lighting device for aquariums. The product integrates advanced RGB LED technology, modern flat design and convenient 3-in-1 features.

## Features

- **Brand**: Neo-Helios
- **Series**: Flat XP Solar Light RGB
- **Features**: 3 in 1 (Light/RGB/Timer)
- **Design**: Flat, modern
- **Technology**: Premium RGB LED

## Application and Usage

### Outstanding Features:
- **RGB**: 16 million adjustable colors
- **Solar Mode**: Simulates natural sunlight
- **Timer**: Automatic on/off scheduling
- **Dimmer**: Smooth brightness adjustment

### Applications:
- Premium planted aquariums
- Shrimp tanks with aquatic plants
- Decorative fish tanks
- Professional aquascaping

### Installation Guide:
1. Place light on tank cover or mounting bracket
2. Connect appropriate power supply
3. Set timer according to lighting cycle
4. Adjust colors and brightness
5. Check remote operation

### Benefits:
- Promotes photosynthesis in aquatic plants
- Creates beautiful lighting effects
- Energy saving with LED technology
- High durability, low maintenance
