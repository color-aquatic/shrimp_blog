# Bluebolt Shrimp

## Introduction

Bluebolt Shrimp is a popular entry-level bee shrimp variety featuring distinctive blue coloration on the head and white body. As part of the Taiwan Bee lineage, Bluebolt can be selectively bred over multiple generations to achieve higher grades such as Deep Blue and Deep Blue Extreme. This affordable and beginner-friendly shrimp is perfect for those starting their journey with bee shrimp varieties.

## Characteristics

- **Scientific name**: Caridina sp. "Bluebolt"
- **Size**: 2-2.5 cm when fully grown
- **Coloration**: Blue head and front section, white body
- **Temperament**: Peaceful, active in small groups
- **Care difficulty**: Easy to Intermediate (beginner-friendly)

## Water Parameters

| Parameter | Recommended Value |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Temperature | 22-26°C |
| pH | 5.0-6.5 |
| TDS | 120-180 ppm |
| GH | 4-6 dGH |
| KH | 1-3 dKH |

## Biological Features

Bluebolt belongs to the Taiwan Bee family and serves as an excellent introduction to the world of bee shrimp keeping. Through selective breeding and genetic mutation, these shrimp can develop into higher-grade variants. Their hardiness and affordable price make them ideal for beginners to gain experience with cold-water and bee shrimp varieties.

## Care Instructions

### Required Equipment

1. **Tank**: Minimum 30cm tank recommended
2. **Substrate**: ADA Gold or Benibachi substrates for optimal pH buffering
3. **Filter**: Gentle biological filtration system
4. **LED lighting**: Moderate lighting sufficient for plant growth
5. **Aquatic plants**: Live moss and aquatic plants for natural filtration

### Diet

- Quality bee shrimp pellets
- Blanched vegetables: Spinach, carrots, pumpkin
- Natural biofilm and algae
- Supplemental foods for color enhancement

## Breeding

Bluebolt shrimp breed readily under proper conditions. Females carry eggs for approximately 25-30 days and can produce 20-35 shrimplets per batch. This species is excellent for learning breeding techniques before advancing to more challenging varieties.

## Special Notes

- **Commercial size**: Sold at 0.8-1.2cm (gender difficult to distinguish at this size)
- **Breeding potential**: Can be upgraded through selective breeding
- **Beginner-friendly**: Ideal starting point for bee shrimp enthusiasts

## Lifespan

1.5-2 years under proper care conditions.
