# Deep Blue Shrimp

## Introduction

Deep Blue Shrimp, also known as Deep Blue Extreme, represents the pinnacle of Bluebolt breeding. This remarkable shrimp displays intense blue coloration from head to tail, unlike regular Bluebolt which only shows blue on the head and back. This full-body blue variant is extremely rare and highly valued by collectors.

## Characteristics

- **Scientific name**: Neocaridina davidi var. "Deep Blue Extreme"
- **Size**: 2-2.5 cm when fully grown
- **Coloration**: Deep blue throughout the entire body from head to tail
- **Temperament**: Peaceful, active in small groups
- **Care difficulty**: Intermediate (requires stable parameters)

## Water Parameters

| Parameter | Recommended Value |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Temperature | 22-26°C |
| pH | 5.0-6.5 |
| TDS | 120-180 ppm |
| GH | 4-6 dGH |
| KH | 1-3 dKH |

## Biological Features

Deep Blue Extreme is the highest grade classification within the Bluebolt lineage. The mutation rate for achieving this phenotype is quite low, requiring high genetic purity and selective breeding. This rarity makes them significantly more expensive than standard Bluebolt varieties.

## Care Instructions

### Required Equipment

1. **Tank**: Minimum 30cm tank recommended
2. **Substrate**: ADA Gold or Benibachi substrates
3. **Filter**: Gentle biological filtration system
4. **LED lighting**: Moderate lighting to enhance blue coloration
5. **Aquatic plants**: Moss and ferns for natural environment

### Diet

- Premium bee shrimp pellets
- Blanched vegetables: Carrots, pumpkin, spinach
- Natural biofilm and algae
- Specialized color-enhancing foods

## Breeding

Deep Blue breeding requires advanced expertise and pristine water conditions. The extreme phenotype has a low occurrence rate, making successful breeding particularly challenging. Females carry eggs for approximately 25-30 days.

## Special Notes

- **Commercial size**: Sold at 0.8-1.2cm (gender difficult to determine at this size)
- **Genetic purity**: Requires careful lineage management
- **Market value**: Premium pricing due to rarity

## Lifespan

1.5-2 years under optimal conditions.
