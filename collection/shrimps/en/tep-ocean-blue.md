# Ocean Blue Shrimp

## Introduction

Ocean Blue Shrimp is an eye-catching ornamental shrimp variety known for cool ocean-like blue tones, ranging from turquoise to deep blue depending on lighting and substrate. It is a great choice for dark aquascapes and moss-focused planted tanks.

## Characteristics

- **Common name**: Ocean Blue Shrimp
- **Size**: 2-2.5 cm when fully grown
- **Coloration**: Ocean blue, turquoise to deep blue
- **Temperament**: Peaceful, active in groups
- **Care difficulty**: Intermediate (requires stable water)

## Water Parameters

| Parameter | Recommended Value |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Temperature | 20-24°C |
| pH | 5.8-6.8 |
| TDS | 110-170 ppm |
| GH | 4-6 dGH |
| KH | 0-2 dKH |

## Care Instructions

### Required Equipment

1. **Tank**: 30cm or larger recommended
2. **Substrate**: Active substrate to keep pH stable
3. **Filter**: Gentle biological filtration with low flow
4. **LED lighting**: Moderate intensity for better coloration
5. **Aquatic plants**: Moss and fine-leaf plants for grazing

### Diet

- High-quality ornamental shrimp pellets
- Blanched vegetables: Spinach, zucchini, carrots
- Natural biofilm and soft algae
- Mineral supplements for healthy molting

## Breeding

Ocean Blue Shrimp can breed consistently in clean, stable conditions. Females usually carry eggs for around 24-30 days. Juveniles often hatch with lighter coloration, which deepens as they mature.

## Special Notes

- Avoid large water changes at once
- Prevent temperature shock during top-up or water changes
- Blue tones usually appear stronger on dark substrate

## Lifespan

Around 1.5-2 years with proper care and stable parameters.
