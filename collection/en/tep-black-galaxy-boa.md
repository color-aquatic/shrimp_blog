# Black Galaxy BOA Shrimp

## Introduction

Black Galaxy BOA Shrimp is the "king of all cold-water shrimp" with ultimate beauty and extremely high value. The BOA (Black on All) pattern combined with pure black color and galaxy-like bright spots creates a living work of art.

## Characteristics

- **Scientific name**: Caridina cf. cantonensis "Black Galaxy BOA"
- **Size**: 2.5-3.2 cm when fully grown
- **Coloration**: Pure black with BOA pattern and galaxy spots
- **Temperament**: Very shy, rarely active during daytime
- **Care difficulty**: Extremely difficult (masters only)

## Water Parameters

| Parameter | Required Value |
|-----------|----------------|
| Temperature | 18-22°C |
| pH | 5.5-6.5 |
| TDS | 80-120 ppm |
| GH | 2-5 dGH |
| KH | 0-1 dKH |

## Biological Features

Black Galaxy BOA represents the pinnacle of shrimp breeding artistry. The BOA (Black on All) pattern means the entire body has deep black coloration, combined with bright galaxy-like spots creating mysterious beauty.

## Care Instructions

### Required Equipment

1. **Tank**: Minimum 80 liters, professional system
2. **Filter**: Premium 3-stage filtration system
3. **Heater**: Titanium heater with precise thermostat
4. **Substrate**: ADA Amazonia or equivalent

### Special Diet

- Premium Japanese shrimp food
- Natural foods: Oak leaves, dried banana leaves
- Enzyme and probiotic supplements

## Breeding

Extremely difficult to breed. Only 5-10 shrimplets per batch, survival rate below 30%.

## Lifespan

3-4 years in perfect environment.
